About Us

We love sea-dipping! Year round we have sea dip sessions every Wednesday & Sunday morning at Renvyle beach. We welcome anyone who would like to embrace the cold. Some of us get in for just a quick dip, others stay in for longer and swim up and down the beach.

We believe that open water swimming is for all ages and abilities of swimmer. From young splashers to older dippers, and everyone in between.

Cold water dipping has numerous health benefits, It can kickstart circulation, which promotes efficient blood flow throughout the body, and can foster mental resilience by challenging the mind to brave the cold! In addition to these benefits, cold water dipping has also been shown to have a radical influence in reducing pain and inflammation, making it a powerful tool for pain reduction. Cold water swimming also releases endorphins, the feel-good hormones, contributing to a mood-boosting afterglow. On top of that, it is a great way of being part of a wonderful community!
